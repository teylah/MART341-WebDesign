hanya yanagihara

Hanya Yanagihara is an American author and editor, best known for her critically acclaimed novel "A Little Life," which explores themes of trauma, friendship, and the complexities of human relationships. Born in Los Angeles, Yanagihara has a diverse background in the arts, having worked in journalism and as the editor-in-chief of T Magazine. Her writing often delves into the emotional depths of characters, making her a prominent and thought-provoking voice in contemporary literature.
